Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for the things most customers want to know before booking.

How long does a mobile oil change take?

Most appointments take about 30 to 45 minutes once service begins. Some vehicles can take longer depending on access, oil capacity, filter location, or skid plates.

Do I need to provide the oil and filter?

No. Full-service packages include oil and filter up to the listed quart amount. The $69 service-only option is available if you prefer to supply your own oil and filter.

Where can the service be performed?

Service can usually be done at a home, workplace, or safe parking location with enough room to work. The vehicle should be parked on a level surface when possible.

Do trucks, SUVs, or luxury vehicles cost extra?

Sometimes. Larger oil capacities, specialty filters, skid plates, luxury vehicles, modified vehicles, or extra travel can affect price. Send vehicle details for an exact quote.

Can you reset the oil life light?

Yes, oil life reset is included when the vehicle supports it and the reset procedure is available.

What should I text to book?

Please send your year, make, model, engine size, service location, and preferred day or time. The booking form can fill that message out for you.

Serving Danville and surrounding Kentucky areas.

Mobile appointments are available for homes, workplaces, and other safe service locations around Danville, Junction City, Perryville, Stanford, Harrodsburg, Lancaster, and nearby areas. Travel availability can vary by distance.
